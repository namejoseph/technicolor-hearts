BIO



Technicolor Hearts was a continually evolving exploration of creativity with a kaleidoscopic array of sound and imagery. Using electronic beats, distorted analog synths, and psychedelic effects, Technicolor Hearts creates a surreal world of avant-garde synth pop. Past band members are Merri Palmer, Carter Greeves (Tambourine Dream), Naomi Cherie and Joseph Salazar.
