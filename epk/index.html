BIO



contact: hellotechnicolorhearts@gmail.com

Technicolor Hearts is a continually evolving exploration of creativity with a kaleidoscopic array of sound and imagery. Using electronic beats, distorted analog synths, and psychedelic effects, Technicolor Hearts creates a surreal world of avant-garde synth pop.

Technicolor Hearts has played Thin Line Festival in Denton, two official showcases at the CMJ Music Marathon in New York and has performed at shows with TOBACCO (of Black Moth Super Rainbow), Dum Dum Girls, Chelsea Wolfe, Zola Jesus, Peaches, Emily Wells, The Pains of Being Pure at Heart, Prince Rama, Julianna Barwick and The Octopus Project.

In early 2015, the band released their second EP Now We're Here which featured songs recorded in Long Beach 2013 with producer Ikey Owens (ex-The Mars Volta, Jack White, Free Moral Agents) who sadly passed away while on tour with Jack White in October of 2014.

Current members: Carter Greeves, Merri Palmer, Joseph Salazar
